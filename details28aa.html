Product Description

Samson Tourniquet is a convenient vaso-constricting device that restricts venous blood flow in the appendages for a short time. It is used for drawing blood samples from veins. It is effective for giving intravenous injections and infusions. In addition, a tourniquet is used for other manipulations where the venous puncture is required.
