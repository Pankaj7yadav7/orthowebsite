NE-0603 - Elastic Knee Support

Elastic Knee Support is a versatile knee support which offers the advantage of knee deloading, lateral immobilization and controlled compression around the knee. It allows normal flexion and free movement of the knee joint

Size : S/M/L/XL/XXL

  • DESCRIPTION
  • ABOUT BRAND

INDICATIONS

  • Patellar Opening
  • Controlled compression
  • Sufficient deloading
  • Free flexion movement

EFFECTIVENESS /TECHNOLOGY

  • Strong Aluminum hinges ensure rigid side splinting but free flexion movement.
  • Open patella design release patellar pressure, hold the patella in position, can be used for Patellofemoral diseases.
  • Wrap design with anterior closing is easy to apply and remove on swollen or asymmetric knees or geriatric patients. Allows customized compression and offers flexibility in sizing.
  • The patellar cushion provides comfortable compression around Patella and improves the grip of the product.
  • Large dorsal opening ensures no buckling on knee flexion, no vasoconstriction on the dorsal area. Improves patient comfort and compliance.
